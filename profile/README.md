AdinCare is a complete digital platform designed for comprehensive management of day centers and continuous follow-up of elderly patients and individuals. It was developed to address a real need: to simplify the work of care staff and guarantee more personalized, safe, and efficient care for each user.

Unlike traditional paper-based or spreadsheet systems, AdinCare centralizes all information in a single digital environment accessible from any device. This allows doctors, nurses, occupational therapists, and caregivers to work in a coordinated manner, sharing real-time updated data.

The platform is designed to improve both the quality of care services and the administrative management of the center, offering tools that range from patient initial registration to daily follow-up of their progress.
